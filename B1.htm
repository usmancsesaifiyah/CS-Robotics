🤔 What is Media Literacy?

It means being a Digital Detective! 🕵️‍♂️

Media Literacy is the ability to look at information, understand what it's saying, and decide if it is helpful, true, or safe.

“Think Before You Click” Rules

✅

Is it TRUE?

Check your facts. Does it come from a source you trust?

🛡️

Is it SAFE?

Never share your password or your real address online!

💖

Is it KIND?

Treat people online the same way you treat them in person.
