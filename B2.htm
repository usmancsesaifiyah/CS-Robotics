Implementation Guide for Teachers

CS Board Decoration Tips:

  • Use a dark matte chart paper for the background to mimic dark-mode IDEs.
  • Print "code snippets" on neon green/cyan paper and scatter them in the background.
  • For Scratch blocks, use foam sheets to give them a raised, tactile feel.
  • Laminate student screenshots to make them look like real computer monitors.

Robotics Board Decoration Tips:

  • Use silver foil paper or metallic wrapping paper for the robot cut-outs.
  • Add bottle caps painted silver for joints and eyes.
  • The "NEW SUBJECT" starburst should be fluorescent yellow card for maximum impact.
  • Glue real (old) circuit boards or wires to the robot for a "pro" look.
